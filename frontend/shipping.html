Shipping Policies

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. During peak seasons, processing may take up to 3 business days.

International Shipping

We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Customs fees and import duties may apply.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order status in your account dashboard.

Shipping Restrictions

Some products may have shipping restrictions due to regulations or carrier policies. We'll notify you if any items in your order cannot be shipped to your location.

Frequently Asked Shipping Questions

When will my order ship?

Most orders ship within 1-2 business days. You'll receive an email confirmation with tracking information once your order has been shipped.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

What if I'm not home for delivery?

If you're not available for delivery, the carrier will leave a notice with instructions for rescheduling or pickup at a local facility.

Can I change my shipping address?

If your order hasn't shipped yet, you can update your shipping address by contacting our customer service team.
